What Goes In

Pure Ingredients,
Honest Craft

🌾 Stone-Ground
Whole Wheat Flour
ઘઉંનો લોટ

Sourced from the sun-drenched plains of Saurashtra. Stone-ground to preserve every fibre and nutrient, giving our khakhras their unmistakable earthy base.

🫙 Cold-Pressed
Sesame Oil
તલનું તેલ

Cold-pressed from white sesame seeds harvested in Gujarat's Kutch district. The oil imparts a rich nuttiness and golden sheen that no refined oil can replicate.

🌿 Hand-Picked
Fenugreek Leaves
મેથી

Sun-dried methi plucked from small family farms around Ahmedabad. Their slight bitterness is the signature note in our most beloved Methi Khakhra.

✨ Slow-Roasted
Cumin & Black Pepper
જીરું & મરી

Dry-roasted in a cast-iron pan until fragrant, then cracked by hand. This unlocks their full aromatic potential before they're folded into the dough.

🧂 Mineral-Rich
Pink Rock Salt
સૈંધવ મીઠું

Sendha namak — the traditional Ayurvedic salt. Mineral-rich and unrefined, it seasons without the harshness of iodized table salt, letting the wheat shine.

❤️ Secret Ingredient
Sonalben's Patience
સ્નેહ

Every khakhra is hand-rolled and slow-roasted on a tawa for a full 20 minutes. You cannot rush authenticity — this is the most important ingredient of all.

The Making

From Grain to Crunch

01
Stone Grind

Wheat is stone-ground at dawn to preserve its natural oils and nutrients.

02
Hand Roll

Each disc is hand-rolled thin with a wooden belan. No machines, no shortcuts.

03
Slow Roast

Roasted on a cast-iron tawa for 20 minutes, pressed constantly to get that signature crunch.

04
Spice & Pack

Hand-seasoned while still warm so spices infuse deep into the crust before sealing.

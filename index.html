Pizza Thepla Wrap

Roll a classic fenugreek-spiced thepla into a delicious wrap filled with capsicum, paneer, oregano, and cream cheese.

Key Ingredients: Thepla, pizza sauce, paneer, capsicum, liquid cheese.

Pizza Thepla Wrap

  1. Heat the thepla on a flat pan with a dab of butter.
  2. Spread 1 tablespoon of pizza sauce evenly down the center.
  3. Add capsicum strips and lightly sautéed paneer cubes.
  4. Drizzle melted cheese and sprinkle pizza spices. Roll tight and serve.

Masala Khakhra Chaat

A crunchy, light street chat. Roughly crushed masala khakhra topped with potatoes, onions, tamarind chutney, and sev.

Key Ingredients: Masala Khakhra, boiled potato, chopped onion, sweet chutney, fine sev.
Tap to see recipe →

Masala Khakhra Chaat

  1. Lightly crush 2 Masala Khakhras into a serving plate.
  2. Layer with cubes of boiled potatoes and chopped raw onions.
  3. Drizzle sweet date-tamarind chutney and spicy green chutney.
  4. Garnish heavily with fine gram flour sev and fresh coriander. Eat immediately.

Khakhra Nachos

Tex-Mex meets Gujarati heritage! Crisp plain khakhra triangles served with black beans, sour cream, and mango salsa.

Khakhra Nachos

  1. Break Plain Khakhras into triangular chip shapes.
  2. Arrange them in a circular pattern on a microwave-safe platter.
  3. Layer with seasoned boiled black beans and shredded cheese.
  4. Heat for 45 seconds to melt cheese. Top with cold sour cream and salsa.

Ratlami Sev Puri

Ratlami Sev Puri

  2. Place a small dollop of mashed spiced potato on each.

  4. Cover the entire assembly with a mountain of coarse Ratlami Sev. Garnish with sour mango bits.

Pantry Chavanu Bhel

Sweet, sour, and crunchy bhel made by tossing puffed rice with raw tomatoes, lemon, and sweet-sour Chavanu.

Key Ingredients: Mix Namkeen, puffed rice (kurmura), chopped tomato, onion, lemon juice.
Tap to see recipe →

Pantry Chavanu Bhel

  1. Mix equal parts puffed rice and Sonalben Mix Namkeen in a large bowl.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes, raw onions, and chopped coriander leaves.
  3. Drizzle 1 tablespoon of raw green mango syrup and fresh lemon juice.
  4. Toss together rapidly and serve in paper cones for the authentic street experience.

Cheese Khakhra Pizza

Cheese Khakhra Pizza

  3. Top with sweet corn kernels, diced bell peppers, and paneer cubes.

Instant Farsan Chaat

A quick dry mixture. Toss Bhavnagari Gathiya and plain sev with roasted peanuts, rock salt, coriander, and fresh lemon.

Key Ingredients: Gathiya, plain sev, peanuts, chopped coriander, lemon juice.
Tap to see recipe →

Instant Farsan Chaat

  1. Mix 1 cup Gathiya and 1/2 cup plain sev in a bowl.
  2. Toss in dry roasted peanuts and fine chopped green chilies.
  3. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the mixture and toss.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve immediately with hot ginger chai.

Travel Snack Packs

Travel Snack Packs

  1. Butter the inner faces of two Jeera Khakhras lightly.
